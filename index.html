As a seasoned forensic anthropologist, my career has been defined by an unwavering commitment to unraveling the mysteries concealed within skeletal remains and contributing to the resolution of complex criminal cases. With a solid foundation in academia and extensive hands-on experience, I bring a unique blend of theoretical knowledge and practical expertise to the field of forensic anthropology.

My journey into this captivating discipline began with a fervent curiosity about the human skeletal system and the stories it can convey. Armed with a Ph.D. in Forensic Anthropology from the prestigious University of California Riverside, my academic pursuits have laid the groundwork for a career dedicated to the application of scientific methodologies in the service of justice.

My passion for forensic anthropology extends beyond the professional realm. I am dedicated to ethical practices within the field, emphasizing the importance of sensitivity and empathy when dealing with human remains. Engaging with local communities, I actively participate in outreach programs to raise awareness about forensic sciences and their role in solving cold cases and bringing closure to families.
